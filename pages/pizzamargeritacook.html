Pizza Margherita

Key Ingredient

San Marzano Tomatoes

San Marzano tomatoes are the gold standard for authentic Neapolitan pizza sauce. Grown in the volcanic soil of Mount Vesuvius, these tomatoes have a perfect balance of sweetness and acidity with fewer seeds and less water than other varieties.

Other ingredients

Mozzarella di Bufala

Fresh Basil

Extra Virgin Olive Oil

00 Flour

Recipe

Prep: 30min
Cook: 15min
Dough Rest: 24h
Total Time: 24h 45min

INGREDIENTS

4 Pizzas

For the Pizza Dough

  • 1kg (7 cups) Tipo 00 flour
  • 600ml (2.5 cups) lukewarm water
  • 20g (2 tbsp) fine sea salt
  • 3g (1 tsp) active dry yeast

For the Toppings

  • 800g San Marzano tomatoes, crushed
  • 400g Mozzarella di Bufala, torn into pieces
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fine sea salt

PREPARATIONS

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

Mix and knead the dough, then allow it to rest for 24 hours in the fridge. Take it out 2 hours before baking.

Step 2: Prepare the Sauce

Use crushed San Marzano tomatoes seasoned with salt. No cooking needed—let it bake fresh on the pizza!

Step 3: Shape and Top the Pizza

Shape dough into circles, spread tomato sauce, add mozzarella. Keep the crust border uncovered.

Step 4: Bake and Finish

Bake at high temp until crust is charred and cheese is melted. Top with basil and olive oil, serve hot.
