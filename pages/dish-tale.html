The Story of Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken originated in Delhi during the 1950s at the famous Moti Mahal restaurant...

The dish is now symbolic of North Indian comfort food, known globally for its creamy, flavorful profile...

It's also a great example of culinary evolution through migration and creativity in the kitchen.
