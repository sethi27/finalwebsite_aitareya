The Story of Pizza Margherita

Pizza Margherita is a classic Neapolitan pizza... named in honor of Queen Margherita of Savoy.

The pizza’s colors represent the Italian flag: red tomatoes, white mozzarella, green basil.

It’s special due to traditional techniques and ingredients like San Marzano tomatoes and buffalo mozzarella.

Recognized by UNESCO in 2017 as an Intangible Cultural Heritage.
